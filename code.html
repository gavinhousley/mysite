JavaScript programming language

In my 40s I made a studious decision to shift careers and take on a Javascript full-stack bootcamp. It is possibly one of the most difficult things I have ever done. But I think my brain will thank me in the future.

I have a GCSE in Computer Studies, which I believe would have been BBC Basic at the time. But I was always fascinated by my brother's computing magazines and how sometimes he would sit and type out the raw code for a game featured in the issue! Later I myself would own an Atari 800XL and even get one with a floppy disk drive.

And so having studied books and music and art all these years, I hope that my knowledge of the history will help me to make interesting things in code - that may not be via the heavily trodden paths that ai is sending us along.
